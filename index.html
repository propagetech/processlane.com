Process Improvement

We follow a systematic approach for process improvements that include Interviewing key stakeholders and employees, Understand the gaps (Gap Analysis) by doing a comparative study and mapping to Industry Best practices, Update policy, Procedures, and templates, Suggest tools, Encourage automation (wherever possible), Close the gaps, Train members (Periodically), Take feedback and Improve.
